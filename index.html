Frequently Asked Questions

What is MoneroSwap?

MoneroSwap is a non-custodial, privacy-focused cryptocurrency exchange. We allow users to swap Bitcoin, Monero, Ethereum, and many other assets instantly without account registration or KYC.

Is it really anonymous?

Yes. We do not collect any personal data, IP logs, or require registration. Our service is designed from the ground up to respect user sovereignty and privacy.

How long does a swap take?

Most transactions are completed within 10-30 minutes. The time depends on the network confirmation speed of the asset you are sending and the current load of the liquidity provider.

What are the fees?

We offer competitive rates with no hidden fees. The amount you see in "You Receive" is the estimated outcome, including all exchange fees. Only the standard network (miner) fee of the destination asset is subtracted.

How do I track my transaction?

After initiating a swap, you will stay on a dynamic tracking page with a unique Transaction ID. You can also bookmark the URL or save the ID to check progress later via our tracking view.
